Microfinance Program Details

Our microfinance initiative provides small business loans ranging from $50 to $500 to women entrepreneurs in rural Bangladesh. The program includes comprehensive financial literacy training, business planning workshops, and ongoing mentorship. With a 95% repayment rate, this program has successfully helped over 2,500 women start sustainable businesses, enabling them to support their families and contribute to their local economies. Loans are offered at low interest rates with flexible repayment terms of 6-12 months.

Healthcare Program Details

Our mobile healthcare program brings essential medical services directly to remote villages through fully-equipped mobile clinics staffed by qualified doctors and nurses. The program focuses on maternal and child health, providing prenatal care, vaccinations, nutrition support, and treatment for common diseases. Serving over 15,000 patients annually, we've achieved a 40% reduction in child mortality in the communities we serve. The clinics operate weekly rotations across 57 districts, ensuring even the most isolated communities receive quality healthcare.

Education Program Details

Our comprehensive education initiative provides scholarships covering full tuition, books, and uniforms for 1,200+ underprivileged children annually. The program also includes school infrastructure development, teacher training workshops, and adult literacy classes for parents. With an 85% graduation rate, we're breaking the cycle of poverty through education. We've built and renovated 15 schools, trained 200+ teachers, and established evening literacy programs that have helped over 800 adults learn to read and write, creating a supportive educational environment for entire communities.

Training Program Details

Our vocational training programs offer 3-month certified courses in tailoring, computer skills, agriculture, and traditional handicrafts. The training centers are equipped with modern tools and led by experienced instructors who provide hands-on learning. With over 800 trainees certified and a 70% job placement rate, we're creating skilled professionals who can earn sustainable incomes. The program includes job placement assistance, business startup support for graduates who want to become entrepreneurs, and ongoing mentorship to ensure long-term success in their chosen fields.
